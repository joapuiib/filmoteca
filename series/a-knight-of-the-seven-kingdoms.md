# A Knight of the Seven Kingdoms

## Sinopsis
A century before GOT, Ser Duncan the Tall, and his squire, Egg, wandered
through Westeros while the Targaryen dynasty ruled the Iron Throne, and dragons
were still remembered. Great destinies and enemies await the incomparable
friends.
